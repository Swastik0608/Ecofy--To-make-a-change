Our product is 99% ecofriendly and they are directly processed from the farms.

Work

Fresh bamboo in forms, cut and sent to factories and manufactures into pencils and seeds are added to distributers, and submitted to retailers at stationary shops and finally reaches to you, as the consumer.


Environmentally friendly products are market-oriented products that cause minimal environmental degradation and their production is linked to a product development process that is structured in a way that considers the impacts that can be caused to the environment throughout their life cycle. The pencils are covered with wood and increasingly with plastic. A bamboo pencil looks, sharpens, feels and writes like a conventional pencil; the user experience is unaltered. Bamboo is a locally available resource and pencil manufacture can be a household enterprise and/or create rural jobs, with a policy change in material specification. Our bamboo pencils are completely eco-friendly, biodegradable and highly durable. They come from nature and go back to it.
